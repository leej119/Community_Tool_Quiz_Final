Introduction

Who this tool is for

For people who keep the atmosphere from breaking

This website is designed for people who often mediate tension, soften their language, stay quiet to avoid escalation, or leave conversations feeling unusually drained.

What it does

The quiz tracks patterns of adjustment, self-silencing, and emotional fatigue. Your answers are saved locally, turned into a score, and used to generate a result with practical next steps.

Project purpose

A small tool, not a perfect solution

This project treats a website as a practical intervention. Instead of only representing polarization as a political issue, it asks how that tension appears in daily life through caution, silence, mediation, and emotional exhaustion.

It is inspired by the idea of useful art: work that can function as a tool, framework, or system people use directly. The goal is to make invisible care work more visible and give users language for naming it.
